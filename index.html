January 6, 2016

Pugs love to play. They like to play outside, inside, in cars, on the grass, in dog parks, in living rooms, in bedrooms, in bathrooms, on porches and decks, in garages and attics, and pretty much anywhere else one can think of. Pugs like to chase balls, play tug of war, chase squirrels and bugs, run around in circles trying to catch their own tail, and even try to catch their shadow. Pugs are curious creatures with very playful hearts.

December 25, 2016

This is a christmas pug. Pugs love the holidays. They like to get presents in the form of treats and other edible things. Pugs like to open packages as well, though sometimes they may need a little bit of help. Pugs do love toys, especially toys that house a little treat inside of them. Pugs like to sip eggnog during Christmas time and have a little bit of the icing from a Christmas cookie. It would be wise to never leave a pug alone near a plate of Christmas cookies, for they will likely eat all of them while you are not looking!
